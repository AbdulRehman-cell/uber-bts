Our Purpose: Making Maritime Travel Effortless

Uber Boats was founded with a bold vision: to simplify and elevate private boat booking for anyone seeking adventure, relaxation, or a seamless connection to stunning destinations. Our mission is to bring the luxury and thrill of boating to your fingertips, wherever your journey leads.

Innovation

We leverage technology to make boat booking as easy as hailing a ride, with transparent pricing and tailored recommendations.

Expertise

Our experienced team and partner crews ensure every journey combines safety, comfort, and world-class service.

Sustainability

We are committed to green practices and eco-friendly vessels, helping preserve waterways for generations to come.

Our Journey

Uber Boats began with a simple question: Why should booking a boat be complicated? Drawing on years of maritime expertise and a passion for innovation, we set out to create a platform that makes ocean, lake, and river journeys accessible to all.

Today, Uber Boats connects travelers, locals, and business clients to a fleet of premium vessels—each crewed by professionals, maintained to the highest standards, and ready to chart the perfect course. Whether you’re celebrating, exploring, or escaping, we’re here to deliver a truly unforgettable experience.
